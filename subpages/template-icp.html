What is ICP?
The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) defines which customers are most likely to buy, succeed with, and retain your product. It is the single most important input across your entire go-to-market strategy.
  • ICP is the company or individual most likely to buy, succeed with, and retain your product
  • The most important input for: demand gen targeting, sales qualification, CS prioritization, product roadmap
The Green/Yellow/Red Framework
Tier every prospect against your ICP using a simple three-band system. This creates a shared language across sales, marketing, and CS for how to prioritize effort.
Green
Target Profile
Actively pursue and qualify in. These customers have the highest probability of success, retention, and expansion.
Yellow
Borderline
Will not disqualify, but require extra scrutiny. May need more resources to succeed. Proceed with caution and heightened CS attention.
Red
Disqualify
Even if they want to buy, disqualify. They will likely churn and hurt unit economics. The cost of serving them exceeds the revenue they generate.

The Retention-Based ICP Discovery Method (Science of Scaling)
The most rigorous way to build your ICP is to work backward from retention data. Start with outcomes, identify the firmographic patterns that predict them, and use those patterns to define your Green profile.
  1. Export all customers with their retention status Pull a complete list of all customers — both active and churned — with their retention status clearly labeled.
  2. Record firmographic data for each customer For each customer, capture: company size, industry/vertical, geography, tech stack, and the buyer's role/level at time of purchase.
  3. Calculate retention rate by each dimension Segment the data by each firmographic dimension and compute the retention rate for each segment independently.
  4. Identify which combinations correlate with high retention Find the intersection of dimensions that together predict the highest retention. This combination defines your Green ICP.

MEDDIC/MEDDPICC Qualification Layer
Beyond firmographic ICP, apply MEDDIC criteria during the sales process to validate deal quality within each tier. ICP tells you whether to engage; MEDDIC tells you how likely the deal is to close.
M
Metrics
Can the buyer quantify the ROI they expect from your solution?
E
Economic Buyer
Have you identified and engaged the person who controls the budget?
D
Decision Criteria
Do you understand and influence the criteria being used to evaluate solutions?
D
Decision Process
Do you understand the steps and timeline to make a decision?
I
Identify Pain
Have you connected their pain to your solution?
C
Champion
Do you have an internal champion who will sell for you when you're not in the room?
Color-Coding Application
Green ICP + Qualified MEDDIC
Fast-track / priority. Assign your strongest reps and accelerate the process.
Yellow ICP + Partial MEDDIC
Proceed with caution. Add CS involvement early and set clear success criteria before signing.
Red ICP — Any MEDDIC
Disqualify regardless of MEDDIC completeness. Revenue from Red ICP customers destroys unit economics.
